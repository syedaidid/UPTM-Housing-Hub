Frequently Ask Question (FAQ)

How do I create an account on the website?

Click on the "Register" button located on the website's navigation bar.

What information do I need to provide when creating a housing advertisement?

Fill out the required fields in the housing advertisement form, including:
1. title
2. description
3. gender
4. number of people
5. Deposit
6. Monthly Payment
7. Utilities include (checkbox)
8. Furnished type
9. Furnished items
10. Facilities
11. Accessibilities
12. Address
12. Images

Can I edit or delete my housing advertisements after posting them?

Log in to your account and navigate your profile. Find the ad you want to edit or delete and just click the edit or delete button to do the action.

How can I search for specific types of housing ads?

Use the search bar or filters provided on the website to narrow down the listings based on criteria such as:
1. Title
2. Gender
3. Number of people
4. Monthly payment range
5. Deposit range

How can I contact the owner or poster of a housing advertisement?

Use the contact information provided in the housing ad, such as email address or phone number, to reach out to the owner or poster.

How often are housing advertisements updated on the website?

Check the website regularly for new listings and updates to existing ones. The ads will be post after it got verified by the administrator

Can I save or bookmark housing ads for future reference?

The functionality for bookmark and saving housing ads is not yet applied in this website. Sorry for the inconvenience

What to do if I forgot my password?

Go to the login page, there is forgot password button at the right of the login button. Insert your email that registered with your account and follow all the step that will be given through email

How do I make a bullet point list for furnished item, facilities and accessibilities

In order to make a bullet point list, use comma(',') after every item you enter
